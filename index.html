Research

Cultural and Orgainzational Assessment of Resource Utilization @ CSULB  June 2023—Aug 2023

Employing applied anthropological methodologies, this assessment investigates a university’s organizational culture to provide insights crucial for designing academic resources and developing impactful strategies to increase student academic success and a sense of belonging on campus. The importance of how a sense of belonging significantly influences student engagement, community building, and overall student success is illustrated by data gathered with qualitative methods. This project provides actionable recommendations for increasing student success and creating environments informed by students' current needs and wants. This assessment underscores the integral role of belonging in DEI efforts and advocates for the practical application of anthropological insights in organizational strategies.
